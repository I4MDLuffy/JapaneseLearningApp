Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kotori ("the app", "we") handles information when you use our Japanese learning application.

1. No Data Collection

Kotori does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no internet permission and makes no network requests of any kind. All data created while using the app stays entirely on your device.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally, solely to provide its features:

  • Saved items — words, kanji, phrases, or other content you save for later study.
  • Known items — content you mark as learned, along with spaced-repetition review data (interval, ease factor, next review date).
  • Kana practice statistics — correct and incorrect counts per kana character, used to track your progress.
  • App settings — your chosen display theme and other preferences.

This data is stored in a local database and shared preferences on your device. It is never uploaded, sold, or shared with anyone.

3. No Third-Party Services

Kotori does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social media services. No SDKs that collect data are included in the app.

4. Children's Privacy

Kotori does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

5. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored only on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
